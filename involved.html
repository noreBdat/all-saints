Service

Being of service can be extremely rewarding and is an essential part of the work we do at All saints at a community and parish level. We urge you to reach out to us and get involved and help us to reach those in need. Volunteers are always needed.

Outreach

All Saints began Outreach in 1997. Since that time we have continued our work with the Zevenfontein and Cosmo City communities providing a soup kitchen, sewing classes and donated food from community sponsors such as Pick n Pay, Fournos and Spar. We also provide assistance with life essentials and work with a registered NPO providing extra lessons in reading, writing and math.
