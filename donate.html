Gratefully Received

Whilst the team at All Saints work tirelessly on our fundraising activities and we are also supported through our monthly tithes and offerings during our services, we are also reliant on the generosity of our community and sponsors. We humbly ask that you consider a donation to All Saints to help us to carry the Lord’s message to our community and continue our outreach programmes. We have a policy of complete transparency in respect of our financial record keeping and you are welcome to access this i nformation on request.

Dedicated Giving

We offer a Dedicated Giving programme at All Saints where members and congregants can commit to a monthly donation. Dedicated Giving is a way of committing yourself to giving on an ongoing basis and this programme is an essential of the planning and budgeting for the church.
