Stepping stones in life

As we journey with God we encounter many life events that bring about great changes both in our personal and spiritual being Many of these life events can be experienced in church and at All Saints we welcome you to embrace them with us.

Confirmations

At All Saints we run confirmation classes for people 16 years and over seeking to make public their personal belief and trust in God. The course is spread over approximately one year. Once completed a life of active worship, witness and service follows as they live into their journey of discipleship.

Weddings

Marriage in the church is a sacrament (outward sign of an inward grace). It is seen as the embodiment of the love between Christ and the Church, and grounds the human relationship in the unconditional love of God. All Saints would love to share this significant milestone with you and yours.

All Saints Baptism

Your child can start their first steps on an amazing journey of faith with God. This special moment can be shared with friends and family

Funerals

When a loved one passes there is always a sense of loss and emptiness. At All Saints we carry a message of hope and love as we remember a loved one in life and a life after death.

Get in Touch and enquire about any of the life events shown above or if you would like to hire our venue for a special occasion or events.
