Faith base initiative

In addition to our traditional ministries and faith based engagement with our members and visitors we have a speacial focus on Youth Ministry and those who prefer less traditional ways of meeting with God.

Youth Ministry

Messy Church is a concept tailored for families who wish to receive God's love in a fun and informal environment. Messy church provides an opportunity for family interaction, conversating and community building. This is done through arts and crafts, activities, worship and a shared meal. It recognizes that our spiritual journey can often be messy

Messy Church meets every Sunday of the month at 1500hrs
